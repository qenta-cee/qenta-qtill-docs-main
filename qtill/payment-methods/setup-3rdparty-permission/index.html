Setting up 3rd party permissions

Setting up 3rd party permissions

Third-party permission to your PayPal Business Account for accessing PayPal-related payment information is needed for integrating PayPal into an online shop.

The recommendation is to set the browser language to English to display all available permissions.

Step 1

If you don’t already have a PayPal account, you need to register a PayPal Business Account at PayPal.

Step 2

Follow the steps as described in the PayPal documentation Grant a third-party permissions using office_api1.qenta.at as the username.

Step 3

On the Add New Third Party Permissions page, select the following types of permissions and click Add.

  • Use Express Checkout to process payments.

  • Issue a refund for a specific transaction.

  • Authorize and capture your PayPal transactions.

  • Obtain information about a single transaction.

  • Charge an existing customer based on a prior transaction.

  • Create and manage Recurring Payments.

  • Use Express Checkout to process mobile payments.

Step 4

Send your PayPal registration e-mail address to our integration specialists.
